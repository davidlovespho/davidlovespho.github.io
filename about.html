  • Who We Are:

    We have been called to minister the Good News of Salvation to the Hmong community in the Fox Valley. We have been part of the Fox Valley since 2003. We have been blessed to witness lives change through the healing power of the Gospel. Our focus is to build relationships within the Body of Christ and help everyone come to know the fullness of Christ.
